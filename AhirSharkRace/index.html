OCEANIC TALES

After conquering the maze and the battlefield, Ahir looked at the deep blue sea.

"What if I was a shark, racing through the ocean and eating everything?" he wondered.

And thus, Shark Race was born.

In this mission, Ahir designed the dynamic speed system where a shark's health dictates its velocity. He architected the aquatic ecosystem, from the harmless tropical fish to the dangerous electric eels and the mighty whales.

Ahir also realized that the ocean isn't always empty‚Äîhumans pass by in boats and sometimes try to defend themselves. This added a tactical layer of risk and reward to the high-speed chase.

This project celebrates the boundless curiosity of an 8-year-old creator, turning a simple race into a strategic survival adventure.
