HOW TO PLAY

🎯 Objective

Move all 4 of your pieces from your Yard to the Home triangle in the center.

🎲 Dice Rolls

Roll a 6 to bring a piece into the hunt. Rolling a 6 also gives you an extra turn!

⚔️ Capturing

Land on an opponent's piece to send it back to their Yard. This also gives you an extra roll!

🏠 Safe Zones

Squares with a star icon (and start squares) are safe! Pieces here cannot be captured.

OUR STORY

One evening, while we were cleaning up the GhostMaze project, Ahir found an old board game in the back of the closet.

"Ludo!" he shouted. "We need this in our game world. But it should have a dark theme and funny pieces!"

Ahir helped design the team colors and came up with the idea of pieces getting "dizzy" after being captured. He also personally curated the AI's trash-talk, wanting it to be cheeky but fair.

Building this together has been a lesson in patience and probability. Ahir loves the tension of needing that "perfect 6."
