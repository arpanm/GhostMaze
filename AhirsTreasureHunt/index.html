MISSION BRIEFING

🎯 The Objective

Navigate the randomly generated terrain to find the main treasure (💎) before your AI opponents do! The faster you find it, the higher your score.

🏃‍♂️ Gameplay & Health

Use Keyboard arrows to move on Desktop, or tap the joystick area on Mobile.

  • Hazards: Beware of wandering ghosts (👻) and stationary traps (🔥). Touching them will drain your health rapidly!
  • Health Potions: Collect green potions (🧪) to restore health.
  • Bonus Loot: Pick up gold coins (💰) for extra points.
  • Death: If your health reaches 0, you lose.

OUR STORY

Ahir wanted a game of exploration, danger, and rewards.

"I want to find treasure in a forest! But there should be ghosts chasing me, and other explorers trying to steal the treasure," he suggested.

We worked together to design the health mechanics and the procedural map generation. Designing the AI bots to wander the map searching for the same goal was a fun challenge. Ahir even made sure there were smaller treasures along the way.
