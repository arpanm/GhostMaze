HOW TO PLAY

🎮 Controls

Move: WASD / Arrow Keys / Joystick

Aim & Fire: Click / Tap on screen

Shield: F key / 🛡️ button

Vehicles: Q (Car) / E (Plane) / R (Sub)

Pause: ESC key / ⏸️ button

🎯 Objective

Lead your 8-member Green Force squad through 30 levels to recover the stolen Emerald Core from the Scarred Red Legion.

Eliminate all enemies in each level to progress. Defeat chapter bosses every 5 levels.

🔫 Weapons

Start with a Pistol. Buy upgrades at the Store with coins earned from kills and loot.

Available: Rifle, Sniper, SMG, Knife, Laser Glasses, Rocket Launcher

Modify weapons with Silencer, Scope, Extended Mag, and Rapid Fire.

🐕 Pets

Your loyal dog auto-attacks nearby enemies and follows you around.

🚗 Vehicles

Car: 2x speed, run over enemies

Plane: Fly over walls

Submarine: Stealth mode (enemies can't see you)

🛡️ Shield

Absorbs damage. Recharges when not in use. Press F to activate.

OUR STORY

From Ghost Maze to Shooting Battle, from Treasure Hunts to War Zones — every game has been a new adventure.

But nothing prepared us for Gun World.

"I want teams, weapons, pets, vehicles, shields, and a real story!" Ahir declared.

His vision was boundless: 30 levels, 6 unique worlds, an army of enemies, and a storyline that would make your heart race.

We built it all — jungle firefights, underwater chases, arctic blizzards, volcanic showdowns. Every level crafted with the imagination of an 8-year-old genius and the determination of a father-son coding team.

This isn't just a game. It's Ahir's world. And you're welcome to explore every corner of it.
