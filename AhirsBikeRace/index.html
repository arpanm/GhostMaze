Bike Race

HOW TO PLAY

OBJECTIVE

Race through different terrains, dodge traffic, fighting off opponents, and avoid the police to reach the finish line in one piece!

CONTROLS

  • Arrow Keys / Touch: Steer Left/Right, Accelerate, Brake
  • Space / Action Button: Attack opponents (Kick/Punch)

RULES OF THE ROAD

  • Combat: Get close to other bikers and hit Attack to knock them down for bonus points. Be careful, they hit back!
  • Crashes: Hitting cars, rocks, or trees will deplete your Health. If Health reaches 0, you crash and respawn (if you have time).
  • The Law: Police cars will chase you if you speed or fight. Don't let them pin you down or you'll get BUSTED!
  • Scoring: Points are awarded for Distance travelled, Speed maintained, and Opponent Takedowns.

THE ORIGIN STORY

Inspired by the adrenaline of classic road racing games, Ahir wanted to recreate that thrill in a modern browser experience. He loves the feeling of speed and the strategy of vehicular combat.

This project is a testament to his passion for gaming and coding. Built with the support of his father, who helped integrate the complex physics engine, debugged the AI opponents, and handled the deployments.
