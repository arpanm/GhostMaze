HOW TO PLAY

🎯 The Objective

Move all 10 of your marbles from your starting triangle to the triangle directly opposite.

🏃 Movement

Standard Move: Slide a marble to any adjacent empty hole.

Jump Move: Hop over an adjacent marble (yours or opponent's) into an empty hole beyond. You can chain as many jumps as you like in a single turn!

💡 Strategy

Create paths of marbles to leap across the entire board in one massive turn.

OUR STORY

After building several action-packed games, Ahir decided he wanted to include a timeless classic in the GhostMaze universe.

"Let's add Chinese Checkers," he suggested. He was drawn to the 121-pointed star board and the deep strategy involved in planning multi-jump moves.

Ahir helped curate the game's personality, suggesting we give the AI a cheeky attitude through "trash talk" and giving the marbles fun, quirky names.

Watching him appreciate the balance of a thousand-year-old game design while adding his own modern, playful touches has been a highlight of our journey.
