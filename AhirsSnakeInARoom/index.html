CREDITS

"Snake in a Room" is a tactical survival game imagined by Ahir.

The concept is simple: Eat or be Eaten.

Ahir wanted a game where you aren't just running away, but actively hunting other snakes. The unique "Encirclement" mechanic was added to reward strategic play - trap your enemies and leave them no escape!

This game requires quick reflexes and a sharp mind. Good luck in the Room!
