OUR STORY

Following the success of our first maze adventure, Ahir decided he wanted something more tactical.

He‚Äôs 8, and his ambition grows with every project.

"Let's make a battle game with soldiers, weapons, and treassures!" he said.

And so, we began a new mission.

In just a few hours, we transformed a blank map into a strategic battlefield. Ahir designed the AI's tactical retreats, the diverse weapon arsenal, and the high-stakes economy.

This project wasn't just about building a shooter; it was about refining the art of digital combat through the eyes of a young creator.

While play-testing the early concepts, Ahir noticed occasions where he was getting stuck or cornered. He realized he needed some "tricks" to escape.

"What if I could deploy false soldiers to confuse the AI?" he asked.

This idea was transformed into our Decoy System, a perfect example of transfer learning from "Ghost Maze" where evasion and timing were key. In this battlefield, Ahir designed not just the arsenal, but the strategic art of misdirection.
