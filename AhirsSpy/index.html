MISSION BRIEFING

🎯 The Objective

There's a party going on, but someone is planning an assassination! Identify and capture the Killer before they terminate the House Owner and steal the priceless necklace.

🕵️ The Investigation

Walk around and mingle. Press SPACE (or tap TALK on mobile) when near someone to interrogate them.

  • Civilians tell the truth. They'll give you visual clues about the Killer or vouch for the innocent.
  • The Killer will lie and may directly blame innocent guests to throw you off!

🚨 The Takedown

When you're sure you've found the culprit, initiate dialogue and use the direct action options:

  • Arrest: Take them into custody. A flawless victory.
  • Shoot: Terminate the threat immediately during conversation!
  • Warning: Arresting or shooting the wrong person will alert the authorities and instantly fail the mission.

OUR STORY

After escaping the Ghost Maze and racing Sharks, Ahir wanted a different kind of challenge.

"What if we make a game where you have to think and solve a mystery instead of just running?" he asked.

And so, Spy Mission was born.

Ahir helped design the core deduction mechanics. He brainstormed the idea that civilians should give visual clues about the killer's traits, like a mustache or glasses, and that the killer would actively try to lie and cast suspicion on innocent bystanders.

He even suggested the ultimate penalty: if you make the wrong deduction and arrest a civilian, your spy gets arrested instead! We worked through the logic of traits, NPC movement, and the interactive questioning system together.

It’s fascinating to see an 8-year-old switch gears from fast-paced action games to a methodical logic puzzle, proving that gameplay design is all about asking the right questions.
