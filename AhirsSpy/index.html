Mission Briefing

🎯 Objective

Identify and eliminate the Killer before they kill the House Owner or steal the Necklace!

🕵️ Your Role

You are the Spy. Everyone looks innocent, but one person is hiding a deadly secret.

🎮 Controls

  • Move: Arrow Keys or Joystick
  • Interact: Walk up to people and press SPACE or click TALK
  • Shoot: Press SHOOT button (or Space if alone)

💡 Tips

  • 🗣️ ASK questions to gather potential clues.
  • 🤥 The Killer might LIE. Civilians tell the truth.
  • 🔫 Be sure before you shoot! Shooting an innocent person calls the Police (Game Over).
  • 💎 Protect the Owner at all costs.
